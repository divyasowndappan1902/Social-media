Frequently Asked Questions

How do I monetize my videos?

Once you reach 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours, you can apply for the Partner Program in your Dashboard.

What are the upload limits?

Basic accounts can upload up to 15 minutes per video. Verified accounts have no length limits and can upload up to 4K resolution.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. We will send a secure reset link to your registered email address.

How can I appeal a copyright strike?

Navigate to the "Copyright" tab in your Studio dashboard and click "Submit Counter Notification" on the affected video.
